Wireless shopping mini update

  •   Fri 18 February 2011
  •   InfoSec

The wheels move slowly when it comes to spending money where I work, but I have just been approved to put in a mini order to test my wireless auditing components, to whit:

1 x Alfa AWUS051NH USB wireless card.

1 x MFJ-1800 2.4 GHz WiFi 15dBi Yagi antenna

1 x 5.8 GHz GRID 24dBi Antenna

1 x RP-SMA male to N Make pigtail cable, 50 Ohm impedance. This is to connect the antennas (N female connector) to the Alfa (RP-SMA female connector)

Fingers crossed.
